I'am Azure

I am Azure, formerly known as Windows Azure. I am Microsoft's public cloud computing platform. I provide a range of cloud services, including compute, analytics, storage and networking. Users can pick and choose from my services to develop and scale new applications, or run existing applications in the public cloud.
